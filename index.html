Privacy Policy for DrawBuddy for skribbl.io

Effective date: November 30, 2025

Information Collection and Use

DrawBuddy reads the words displayed on skribbl.io in your browser in order to provide helpful reference images for drawing. No personal data, location, web history, authentication information, or other sensitive information is collected or transmitted.

Data Processing

All processing happens locally in your browser. The extension only stores your preferences, specifically whether the extension is enabled or disabled, using Chrome's storage API.

Third-Party Services

DrawBuddy does not send your data to any third-party servers. If you enable optional features like submitting unknown words to improve the dataset, no personal information is included.

Security

We follow standard practices to protect your data locally in the browser. Since no personal data is collected, there is minimal risk.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Updates will be reflected on this page.
